• What is this shiz?

    It's a simple and lightweight Kanban board.

  • How do I add a new card/task?

    Double click anywhere inside the column.

  • Where is the data being stored?

    The data is only being stored in your browser (local storage). As long as you don't delete it, it will stay there.

  • Did you use the kanban board to plan and execute this very project?

    Yes! It was a very effective "eat your own dog food" experience.

  • Principles

    • Limit your WIP (Work In Progress)
    • Do not prioritize tasks - instead use a column for things you might do, and a column for things you are going to do next
    • No fields/metadata: Everything about the task should be in its title and description
